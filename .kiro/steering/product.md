# TDS Web Simulation - Product Overview

Interactive web-based visualization and simulation of the Theory of Dynamic Symmetry (TDS), a theoretical physics model exploring symmetry dynamics, anomalies, and reversible lattice dynamics.

## Core Features

- **Interactive Lattice Visualization**: Real-time 2D and 3D rendering of lattice structures with node states and energy flows
- **Time-Reversible Simulation**: Full forward/backward playback with history tracking and bookmarking
- **Physics Problems Interface**: Educational testing environment for TDS concepts
- **Analytics Dashboard**: Real-time metrics, entropy calculations, and correlation length tracking
- **Tutorial System**: Guided tours and educational content
- **Multi-language Support**: English and Russian localization
- **PWA Support**: Offline-capable progressive web app

## Key Concepts

- **Nodes**: Lattice points with states (symmetric, broken, anomaly) and energy levels
- **Symmetry Dynamics**: Evolution of symmetry breaking and restoration
- **Anomaly Propagation**: Wave-like spread of anomalies through the lattice
- **Reversibility**: Complete time-reversible dynamics with state history
- **Bookmarks**: Save and restore interesting simulation moments

## Target Users

Physics students, researchers, and educators interested in symmetry theory, lattice dynamics, and theoretical physics visualization.
